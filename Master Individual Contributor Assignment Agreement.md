# Master Individual Contributor Assignment Agreement

This Master Individual Contributor Assignment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **SAAScade LTD**, a company incorporated in The Republic of Ireland ("Company"), and the individual accepting or signing this Agreement ("Contributor").

Effective as of the date of electronic acceptance or signature ("Effective Date").

---

## 1. Scope and Application

This Agreement governs all Contributions submitted before, on, or after the Effective Date.

It applies to all repositories, projects, products, services, and materials owned or controlled by Company, whether now existing or created in the future.

This Agreement is executed once and applies to all present and future Contributions.

---

## 2. Definitions

"Contribution" means any source code, object code, documentation, inventions, discoveries, improvements, ideas, feedback, data, specifications, or other materials submitted or provided by Contributor to Company by any means.

---

## 3. Assignment of Intellectual Property

Contributor irrevocably assigns to Company all worldwide right, title, and interest in and to each Contribution, including:

- All copyrights  
- All rights to register and enforce copyrights  
- All rights to sue for past, present, and future infringement  
- All rights to prepare derivative works  
- All associated goodwill  

To the extent permitted by applicable law, Contributions shall be deemed works made for hire.

If not deemed works made for hire, they are assigned under this Section.

Contributor agrees to execute documents reasonably necessary to perfect or enforce Company’s rights worldwide.

---

## 4. Patent Assignment

Contributor irrevocably assigns all worldwide patent rights embodied in or relating to the Contributions.

If assignment is not permitted under applicable law, Contributor grants Company a perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free, transferable, sublicensable license.

---

## 5. Moral Rights Waiver

To the maximum extent permitted under Irish and applicable EU law, Contributor waives and agrees not to assert any moral rights in the Contributions.

Where waiver is not legally permitted, Contributor agrees not to enforce such rights against Company or its licensees.

---

## 6. Representations and Warranties

Contributor represents and warrants that:

- Contributor has full authority to enter into this Agreement.
- Contributions are original or properly authorized.
- Contributions do not infringe third-party rights.
- No Contribution is subject to conflicting license obligations.
- Required employer permissions have been obtained where applicable.

---

## 7. Indemnification

Contributor shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Company and its affiliates, directors, officers, employees, successors, and assigns from and against any claims, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including reasonable legal fees) arising from:

- Breach of this Agreement;
- Intellectual property infringement claims relating to Contributions;
- Failure to obtain necessary permissions.

This obligation survives termination.

---

## 8. Data Protection

Company may process Contributor’s identifying information for intellectual property record-keeping and enforcement purposes in compliance with GDPR and Irish law.

---

## 9. Binding Arbitration (JAMS)

Any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved by final and binding arbitration administered by JAMS under its Comprehensive Arbitration Rules.

Seat of arbitration: Dublin, Ireland  
Language: English  
One arbitrator.

Company may seek interim or injunctive relief in the Irish courts for protection of intellectual property.

---

## 10. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of The Republic of Ireland.

---

## 11. Survival

Intellectual property ownership, patent rights, indemnification, arbitration, and governing law provisions survive termination.

---

## 12. Execution; Electronic and Manual Signatures

This Agreement may be accepted by:

- Click-through acceptance;
- Digital signature;
- Qualified electronic signature under eIDAS;
- Manual (wet ink) signature.

Scanned or electronically transmitted copies of manually signed versions shall be deemed original and legally binding.

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.
