# Corporate Contributor Assignment Agreement

This Agreement is entered into between **SAAScade LTD**, incorporated in The Republic of Ireland ("Company"), and                                      ("Contributor Company").

Effective as of the date signed below.

---

## 1. Assignment

Contributor Company irrevocably assigns to Company all worldwide right, title, and interest in Contributions submitted by its authorized employees or contractors.

---

## 2. Patent Rights

Contributor Company assigns all worldwide patent rights embodied in such Contributions.

If assignment is not permitted, Contributor Company grants Company a perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free, transferable, sublicensable license.

---

## 3. Representations

Contributor Company represents that:

- It has authority to enter this Agreement.
- Authorized individuals are properly engaged.
- Contributions do not infringe third-party rights.
- Required permissions have been obtained.

---

## 4. Indemnification

Contributor Company shall indemnify and hold harmless Company and its affiliates from any claims, damages, liabilities, or expenses arising from breach or infringement related to Contributions.

This obligation survives termination.

---

## 5. Binding Arbitration (JAMS)

Disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration administered by JAMS.

Seat: Dublin, Ireland  
Language: English  

Company may seek interim relief in Irish courts for intellectual property protection.

---

## 6. Governing Law

The laws of The Republic of Ireland govern this Agreement.

---

## 7. Execution

This Agreement may be executed manually, electronically, or in counterparts.

Scanned copies of signed versions shall be legally binding.
